About

I’m an aquatic ecologist interested broadly in water quality, enviromental change, and data science. My research focuses on ecosystem resilience and regime shifts, algal blooms, and metabolism. I’m currently a Ph.D. candidate in the Pace Lab at the University of Virginia’s Department of Environmental Sciences. I completed my B.S. in 2014 at the University of Wisconsin in Biology and Mathematics.
